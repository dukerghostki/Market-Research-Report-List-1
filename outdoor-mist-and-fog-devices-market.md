<p><p>The outdoor mist and fog devices market is primarily segmented into low-pressure and high-pressure devices. Low-pressure devices operate at lower energy levels, typically using less water and producing a coarser mist, making them ideal for residential or recreational use. High-pressure devices, on the other hand, generate a finer mist by utilizing higher pressure, resulting in better evaporation and cooling effects, making them suitable for commercial applications like outdoor dining, industrial cooling, and large-scale landscaping. Both types enhance comfort and ambiance in outdoor settings.</p></p>
