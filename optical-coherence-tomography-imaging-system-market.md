<p><ul><li>Time-Domain Type</li><li>Spectral-Domain Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) imaging system market is primarily divided into two types: Time-Domain OCT (TD-OCT) and Spectral-Domain OCT (SD-OCT). TD-OCT measures the time delay of light reflected from tissue, providing lower resolution and slower image acquisition. In contrast, SD-OCT captures the spectral data of light, allowing for higher resolution and faster imaging, making it more prevalent in clinical applications. These distinctions cater to varying diagnostic needs and imaging quality in medical settings.</p></p>
