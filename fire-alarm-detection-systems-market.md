<p><strong>The Fire Alarm Detection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Ionization</li><li>Dual Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire Alarm Detection Systems can be categorized into three main types: Photoelectric, Ionization, and Dual Sensor. Photoelectric alarms utilize a light source and a sensor to detect smoke from smoldering fires, making them effective for slower-burning fires. Ionization alarms use radioactive materials to detect fast-flaming fires through changes in ion currents. Dual Sensor alarms combine both technologies, enhancing detection capabilities by responding to a broader range of fire types. This variety ensures effective protection in diverse environments.</p></p>
