<p><p>The patient scales market is categorized into two main types: mechanical and electronic scales. Mechanical patient scales operate through a system of springs and levers, providing a manual method for weight measurement. They are typically more durable and do not require power sources. In contrast, electronic patient scales use digital technology to deliver precise weight readings and often come with additional features such as BMI calculation and memory storage. Both types serve essential roles in healthcare settings for accurate patient monitoring.</p></p>
