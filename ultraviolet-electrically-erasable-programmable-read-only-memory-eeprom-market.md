<p><p>Ultraviolet Electrically Erasable Programmable Read Only Memory (EEPROM) is a non-volatile memory technology allowing data to be erased and reprogrammed using ultraviolet light. The market for EEPROM can be categorized into Surface Mounted and Through Hole types. Surface Mounted EEPROMs are compact, allowing for higher density and automated assembly processes, making them ideal for modern electronics. Conversely, Through Hole EEPROMs feature leads inserted into drilled holes on the circuit board, providing robust connections, suitable for older or industrial applications.</p></p>
