<p><p>Encoders for robots are vital sensors that convert mechanical motion into electrical signals, aiding in precise control and positioning. Absolute encoders provide unique position information for each shaft angle, ensuring accurate feedback even after power loss. In contrast, relative encoders, or incremental encoders, measure changes in position, providing data based on movement from a defined starting point. The market for these encoders is driven by their applications in automation, robotics, and industrial processes, enhancing efficiency and precision in operations.</p></p>
