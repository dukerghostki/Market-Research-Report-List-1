<p><p>Insulating Concrete Forms (ICF) are construction components made from a balance of rigid foam insulation and concrete that provide structural integrity and thermal efficiency. The market is segmented into three main types: Block, Panel, and Plank. Block ICFs are modular, interlocking units that create walls. Panel ICFs consist of larger sheets used for faster installations. Plank ICFs offer flexibility in design, mimicking traditional framing while maintaining insulation properties. Each type caters to diverse building needs while enhancing energy efficiency.</p></p>
