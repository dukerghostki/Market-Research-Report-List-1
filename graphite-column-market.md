<p><p>The graphite column market includes several types of columns used for various separation processes. Bubble-cap columns feature caps to create bubbles, facilitating mass transfer between liquids and gases. Sieve tray columns employ perforated trays to promote vapor-liquid interaction, optimizing separation efficiency.
