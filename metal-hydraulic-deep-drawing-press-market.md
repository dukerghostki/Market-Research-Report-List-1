<p><ul><li>Single Action System</li><li>Double Action System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metal Hydraulic Deep Drawing Press market is categorized into two main types: Single Action System and Double Action System. The Single Action System utilizes a single hydraulic cylinder for pressing metal sheets into molds, making it suited for simpler designs and thinner materials. In contrast, the Double Action System features two hydraulic cylinders, allowing for more complex shapes and thicker materials by providing greater control and pressure during the forming process. Both systems cater to different production needs in the metalworking industry.</p></p>
