<p><p>The Voyage Data Recorder (VDR) market is primarily divided into two types: General Voyage Data Recorder (G-VDR) and Simplified Voyage Data Recorder (S-VDR). G-VDRs are designed for comprehensive data collection, including various parameters like bridge audio, navigation position, and engine data, making them suitable for larger vessels. In contrast, S-VDRs offer a more streamlined solution, capturing essential information necessary for investigations while being more cost-effective and easier to install, often appealing to smaller ships and operators with budget constraints.</p></p>
