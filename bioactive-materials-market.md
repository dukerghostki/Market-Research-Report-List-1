<p><p>The bioactive materials market is categorized into three main types: powders, moldable materials, and granules. Powders are fine particles that can be blended with other substances for various biomedical applications, such as coatings or implants. Moldable materials offer flexibility and can be shaped for specific applications, often used in tissue engineering and surgical repairs. Granules consist of larger particles, ideal for controlled release systems and filling voids in bone repair, allowing for effective integration with biological tissues.</p></p>
