<p><p>Hot Bonder market types refer to the configurations of heating systems used for composite bonding in aerospace and other industries. In a Single Zone market, the equipment operates within one temperature-controlled area, ensuring uniform heating across the bonded surface. Both types enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of adhesive curing in various applications.</p></p>
