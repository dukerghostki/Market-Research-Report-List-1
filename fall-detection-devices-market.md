<p><p>The fall detection devices market comprises three main types: wearable systems, in-home landline systems, and in-home cellular systems. In-home landline systems utilize existing home telephone connections to notify emergency services when a fall occurs, while in-home cellular systems leverage mobile networks for communication, providing more flexibility and wider coverage for users who may not have landlines.</p></p>
