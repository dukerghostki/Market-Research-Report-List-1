<p><strong>The Commercial Aircraft Landing Gear Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strut Landing Gear</li><li>Rocker Landing Gear</li><li>Pontoon Landing Gear</li><li>Framed Landing Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial aircraft landing gear market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Strut Landing Gear** utilizes vertical struts and is commonly found in small aircraft, providing durability and simplicity.</p><p>2. **Rocker Landing Gear** features a pivoting mechanism, allowing for better shock absorption during landing.</p><p>3. **Pontoon Landing Gear** is designed for seaplanes, enabling landings on water, while providing buoyancy and stability.</p><p>4. **Framed Landing Gear** consists of an external framework supporting the wheels, often used in larger aircraft for enhanced strength and weight distribution. Each type serves specific operational needs and aircraft designs.</p></p>
