<p><strong>The Silicone Rubber for Electronic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RTV</li><li>HTV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone rubber in the electronic market is primarily categorized into two types: Room Temperature Vulcanizing (RTV) and High-Temperature Vulcanizing (HTV) silicone rubber. RTV silicone cures at room temperature, making it ideal for quick repairs and assembly. It offers excellent electrical insulation and flexibility. In contrast, HTV silicone requires heat to cure and is favored for applications demanding high-temperature resistance and durability. Both types provide exceptional thermal stability and are widely used in potting, encapsulation, and insulation within electronic devices.</p></p>
