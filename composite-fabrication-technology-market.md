<p><strong>The Composite Fabrication Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lay-up</li><li>Filament Winding</li><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Pultrusion</li><li>Compression Molding</li><li>RTM</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Composite Fabrication Technology encompasses various techniques used to create composite materials. Lay-up involves stacking layers of materials, ideal for complex shapes. Filament Winding reinforces structures by winding filaments around a mold. Injection Molding allows for intricate designs by injecting resin into molds. Pultrusion continuously pulls fibers through a resin bath, forming strong profiles. Compression Molding uses heat and pressure to shape materials, while RTM (Resin Transfer Molding) injects resin into a closed mold. Other methods include autoclave processing and additive manufacturing.</p></p>
