<p><ul><li>Balloon Kyphoplasty Devices</li><li>Vertebroplasty Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vertebral compression fracture devices market includes two primary types: balloon kyphoplasty devices and vertebroplasty devices. Balloon kyphoplasty involves the insertion of a balloon into the fractured vertebra to restore height before the injection of a cement-like material, providing stabilization and relief from pain. Vertebroplasty, on the other hand, entails directly injecting the bone cement into the fractured vertebra without prior balloon expansion. Both techniques aim to alleviate pain, improve mobility, and enhance the quality of life for patients suffering from spinal fractures.</p></p>
