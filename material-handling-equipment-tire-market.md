<p><strong>The Material Handling Equipment Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Solid</li><li>Polyurethane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Material handling equipment tires are classified into three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane. Pneumatic tires feature air-filled chambers, providing superior shock absorption and traction for rough terrains, ideal for outdoor applications. Solid tires, made from resilient rubber, eliminate the risk of punctures, making them perfect for indoor use or heavy-duty operations. Polyurethane tires combine durability and low rolling resistance, suitable for warehouse and pallet jack applications, offering extended wear life and enhanced performance on smooth surfaces.</p></p>
