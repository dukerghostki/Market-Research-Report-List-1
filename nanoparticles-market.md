<p><p>The nanoparticles market encompasses various types, each serving unique applications. Liposomes facilitate drug delivery by encapsulating therapeutic agents within lipid bilayers. Polymer-conjugated drugs enhance solubility and target delivery via polymers attached to drugs. Polymeric nanoparticles offer controlled release and improved stability. Dendrimers have a branched structure for precise drug targeting. Inorganic nanoparticles, such as gold and silver, provide diagnostic and therapeutic benefits. Other categories may include micelles and nanoemulsions, broadening the scope of advanced drug delivery systems.</p></p>
