<p><p>The urology drugs market includes various treatment options for urinary tract and prostate conditions, prominently featuring alpha blockers and 5-alpha reductase inhibitors. Alpha blockers are primarily used to relieve urinary symptoms associated with benign prostatic hyperplasia by relaxing muscles around the bladder and prostate. In contrast, 5-alpha reductase inhibitors work by reducing dihydrotestosterone levels, leading to prostate shrinkage and symptom relief. Both classes are essential in managing conditions affecting urinary function and prostate health.</p></p>
