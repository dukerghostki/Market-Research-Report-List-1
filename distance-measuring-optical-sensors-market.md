<p><ul><li>Laser</li><li>LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distance measuring optical sensors are categorized primarily into laser and LED types. Laser-based sensors utilize coherent light to achieve high precision and long-distance measurements, making them suitable for industrial and survey applications. Meanwhile, LED-based sensors employ light-emitting diodes to measure distances, typically providing lower accuracy and range but are cost-effective for shorter distances and general use. Both types find applications in various sectors, including construction, robotics, and automotive industries, each offering distinct advantages based on specific measurement needs.</p></p>
