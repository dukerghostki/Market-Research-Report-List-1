<p><p>The Triple Duty Valve market is segmented into two main types: threaded and flanged valves. Threaded valves feature male or female threads for secure connections, making them ideal for compact spaces and easy installation. Flanged valves use flat surfaces with bolt holes for mounting, providing robust connections suitable for larger pipes and higher pressure applications. Both types facilitate flow control, pressure regulation, and system isolation, catering to varied industrial needs in plumbing, HVAC, and process industries.</p></p>
