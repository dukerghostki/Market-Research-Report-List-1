<p><p>The ICP Optical Spectrometer market is divided into two main types: Sequential and Simultaneous. The Sequential type conducts analyses one element at a time, which allows for high-resolution measurements but can be time-consuming. Conversely, the Simultaneous type analyzes multiple elements simultaneously, offering quicker results and enhanced efficiency. Both types serve crucial roles in various applications, including environmental monitoring, materials testing, and quality control, catering to different needs based on the desired analysis speed and resolution.</p></p>
