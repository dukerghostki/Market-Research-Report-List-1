<p><p>Telecom Cloud Billing Services encompass various market types tailored to meet diverse customer needs. Convergent Billing consolidates multiple service charging into a unified framework, simplifying management. D2C (Direct-to-Consumer) Billing focuses on direct transactions between service providers and users, enhancing customer engagement. Prepaid Billing allows customers to pay in advance for services, promoting better budget control and reducing credit risks for service providers. Each type enhances operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.</p></p>
