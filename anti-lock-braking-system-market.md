<p><p>The Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) market comprises two main types: pneumatic ABS and hydraulic ABS. Pneumatic Anti-Lock Braking Systems utilize air pressure to modulate brake force, enhancing stability during sudden stops and slippery conditions, commonly used in commercial vehicles. In contrast, Hydraulic Anti-Lock Braking Systems rely on hydraulic fluid to achieve similar outcomes, providing precise brake control and improved safety. Both systems aim to prevent wheel lock-up, thereby increasing vehicle control and reducing stopping distances under various driving conditions.</p></p>
