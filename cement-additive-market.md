<p><p>The cement additive market comprises three primary types: fiber, chemical, and mineral additives. Fiber additives enhance the tensile strength and ductility of cementitious products while reducing cracking. Chemical additives, such as retarders and superplasticizers, improve workability and control setting times, leading to enhanced performance. Mineral additives, including fly ash and silica fume, optimize durability and sustainability by recycling materials and improving the cement's properties. Together, these additives contribute to the performance and efficiency of cement in construction applications.</p></p>
