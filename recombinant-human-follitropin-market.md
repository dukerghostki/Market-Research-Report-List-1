<p><p>The Recombinant Human Follitropin market comprises three main types: RHF-α, RHF-β, and Urofollitropin. RHF-β, similar in function, emphasizes distinct structural properties that may enhance efficacy. Urofollitropin, derived from human urine, contains both FSH and LH and is used in fertility treatments. Each type caters to specific patient needs and treatment scenarios in reproductive medicine.</p></p>
