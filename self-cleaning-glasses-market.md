<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic Coating</li><li>Hydrophilic Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-cleaning glasses market is primarily divided into two types based on their coatings: hydrophobic and hydrophilic. Hydrophobic coatings repel water and dirt, allowing raindrops to roll off and carry away contaminants, making surfaces easier to clean. In contrast, hydrophilic coatings attract water, spreading it into a thin film that helps dissolve dirt and grime for effortless cleaning. These innovative coatings enhance the durability and maintenance of glass surfaces in various applications, including architecture, automotive, and solar panels.</p></p>
