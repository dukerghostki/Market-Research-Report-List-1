<p><ul><li>Anatomical Shoulder Prosthesis</li><li>Reverse Shoulder Prosthesis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shoulder replacement devices market primarily comprises two types: anatomical shoulder prosthesis and reverse shoulder prosthesis. Anatomical shoulder prosthesis mimics the natural structure of the shoulder, catering to patients with intact rotator cuffs needing reconstruction. In contrast, reverse shoulder prosthesis alters the shoulder's mechanics, allowing patients with severe rotator cuff deficiencies or patients who have failed previous surgeries to regain mobility. Both types aim to alleviate pain and restore functionality, addressing different patient needs and anatomical conditions.</p></p>
