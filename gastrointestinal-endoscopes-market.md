<p><p>The gastrointestinal endoscopes market consists of two main types: flexible and rigid endoscopes. Flexible endoscopes are designed for easier navigation through the bends of the digestive tract, allowing for minimally invasive procedures and enhanced visualization. Rigid endoscopes, on the other hand, offer stability and precision for certain diagnostic and surgical applications but are limited in maneuverability. Both types are essential for gastrointestinal diagnostics, therapeutic interventions, and advancements in minimally invasive surgical techniques, catering to varying clinical needs.</p></p>
