<p><p>The pharmaceuticals excipients market encompasses various types of substances that aid in drug formulation. Binders enhance the adhesion of powders, ensuring tablet integrity. Glidants improve the flow properties of powders, facilitating uniformity in dosage. Diluents are used to increase the bulk volume of formulations, making them easier to handle and consume. Disintegrants promote tablet breakdown into smaller fragments, ensuring quick release of the active ingredient. Together, these excipients play crucial roles in the efficacy and manufacturability of pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
