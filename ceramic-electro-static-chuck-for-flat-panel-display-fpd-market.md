<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic Electrostatic Chucks (ESCs) are essential tools in the fabrication of Flat Panel Displays (FPDs), ensuring precise substrate positioning during processing. The Coulomb type operates by generating an electric field that holds the substrate through electrostatic force, providing stable support. In contrast, the Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type utilizes a combination of electrostatics and surface forces, allowing for better grip and uniform pressure distribution. Both types enhance manufacturing efficiency and quality by minimizing defects in the delicate FPD manufacturing process.</p></p>
