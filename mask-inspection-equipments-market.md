<p><strong>The Mask Inspection Equipments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mask inspection equipment market is primarily divided into two methods: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The DD method inspects masks by comparing one die to another, ensuring precision in features crucial for semiconductor fabrication. Conversely, the DB method compares a mask against a predefined database of known good patterns, streamlining the detection of defects. Both methods are essential for maintaining yield and quality in semiconductor manufacturing, each serving distinct inspection needs in the production process.</p></p>
