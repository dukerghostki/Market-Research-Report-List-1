<p><p>Flaw detectors are essential tools used for identifying imperfections in materials. The market is primarily divided into three types: Magnetic flaw detectors, which utilize magnetic fields to identify surface and subsurface defects in ferromagnetic materials; Ultrasonic flaw detectors, which employ high-frequency sound waves to detect internal flaws in various materials; and Others, which encompass technologies such as eddy current and acoustic emission detectors. Each type serves specific applications depending on the material and the nature of defects to be examined.</p></p>
