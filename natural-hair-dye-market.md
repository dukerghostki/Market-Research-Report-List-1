<p><p>The natural hair dye market is segmented into three main types: </p><p>1. **Temporary Hair Dye**: Offers short-lasting color that typically rinses out after a few washes, ideal for special occasions or experimentation.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Semi-Permanent Hair Dye**: Lasts for several weeks, gradually fading with washes, and provides a richer color without the commitment of permanent dye.</p><p>3. **Demi-Permanent Hair Dye**: Lasts longer than semi-permanent, usually staying vibrant for up to 24 washes, using a low-level developer to penetrate the hair shaft without harsh chemicals.</p><p>4. **Permanent Hair Dye**: Provides long-lasting color, often requiring a grow-out period, and effectively covers grey hair. </p><p>These types cater to various consumer preferences and hair care needs.</p></p>
