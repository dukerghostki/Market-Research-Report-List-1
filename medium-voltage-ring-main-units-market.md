<p><ul><li>Solid Insulated</li><li>Gas Insulated</li><li>Air Insulated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medium Voltage Ring Main Units (RMUs) are crucial for electrical distribution networks, categorized into three types: Solid Insulated, Gas Insulated, and Air Insulated. Solid Insulated RMUs employ high-permittivity materials for insulation, offering compact designs and enhanced safety. Gas Insulated RMUs utilize sulfur hexafluoride (SF6) gas, providing superior insulation and minimal footprint, ideal for urban environments. Air Insulated RMUs rely on air as the primary insulating medium, suitable for less confined spaces and often more cost-effective, though larger in size.</p></p>
